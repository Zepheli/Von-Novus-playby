Von Novus

6'0, Horned Drakanite
Lazy yet often dedicated to the idea of putting in as little work as possible Von Novus is a Squire Of The First Light under the tutelage of Thim, his exact origins are unknown, however he has been referenced to have some relationship with the wilderness. He currently lives in Osrona, though what land he owns is not yet known, if any.
